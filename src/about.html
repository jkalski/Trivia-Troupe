About TriviaTroupe

TriviaTroupe is a fun, fast-paced, and mildy customizable quiz game designed to test your knowledge across a range of topics like Math, Science, History, and Pop-Culture.

🎯 Our Mission

To make learning engaging, accessible, and entertaining for everyone—whether you're studying or just flexing your trivia muscles.

💡 Features

  • Multiple built-in trivia categories
  • Quick game sessions to test your knowledge with no delay
  • Progress tracking and history
  • Tutorial for new users
  • Category creation and customization

👨‍💻 Built By

TriviaTroupe was created with care by an enthusiastic team of developers who love trivia and learning. This project was created, primarily, for an intro to software engineering projects class and serves to demonstrate basic knowledge of both backend and frontend development in a semi-proffesional setting. Trivia Troupe's frontend is built using Html, CSS, and Javascript and our backend is built utilizing Flask and MongoDB.
